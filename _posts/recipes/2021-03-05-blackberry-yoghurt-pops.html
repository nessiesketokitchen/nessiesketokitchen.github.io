This is the only treat you'll need for summer, it's perfectly creamy yet still light and refreshing. It's packed full of probiotics and low in sugar, who knew healthy treats could be this good?

Prep time: 5 minutes
Cook time: 15-20 minutes
Serving: 5 pops

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup frozen blackberries
  • 1/3 cup erythritol
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1 1/2 cup full/cream double cream yoghurt

Recipe


  1. Combine the blackberries, erythritol and water in a sauce-pan and leave to simmer over a medium heat.
  2. Let it reduce for 15-20 minutes, you want it to thicken slightly.
  3. Leave the mix to cool down.
  4. Blend the blackberries compote and yoghurt together. Alternatively you can mix it by hand for a more chunky pop.

Tip

To give your gut the best nutrition possible, use good quality yoghurt that is free from preservatives and additives.

Enjoy!
