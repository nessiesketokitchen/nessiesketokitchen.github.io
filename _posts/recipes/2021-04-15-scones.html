Invite your friends over for coffee because they are going to love these. They are crumbly, soft and deliciously nutty. Who knew cutting carbs could be this easy?

Prep time: 10 minutes
Cook time: 12-15 minutes
Serving: 15 scones

Ingredients

  • 4 cups almond/macadamia flour
  • ¼ cup whey powder
  • ⅓ cup erythritol
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 100g butter, cold
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup kefir/buttermilk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions


  1. Preheat the oven to 190Â°C and line a baking tray.
  2. Combine the dry ingredients in a mixing bowl.
  3. Cut the butter into small chunks and rub into the dry ingredients using the tip of your fingers.
  4. Combine the wet ingredients and add it to the dry ingredients.
  5. Shape the mixture into little cakes, see picture for reference (+- 1cm thick/6cm diameter).
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes on the prepared tray, until golden brown.



Tip

Serve with jam, cheese and cream for a more traditional scone.
Other serving ideas include: lemon curd, custard or syrup.
These scones are so tasty, I mostly enjoy having them with just butter.
Remember to adjust the baking time if you change the size of the scones.
