I grew up eating a lot of baked beans but with all the added sugar in commercially produced beans, it's something I had to give up. Legumes are a great source of fiber but the problem lies with all the sugar found in the sauce so I set out to recreate one of my favourite childhood meals.

Prep time: 5 minutes
Cook time: 17 minutes
Serving: equal to 1 store bought can of beans

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup stock
  • 1 tbsp erythritol
  • 3 drops liquid smoke
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 can butter beans, drained

Instructions


  1. In a small pot, combine all the ingredients except the butter beans. Cook on a medium heat until it starts to simmer (1-2 minutes).
  2. Add the butter beans and cook uncovered for 15 minutes or until the sauce has thickened.
  3. Remove from the heat and serve warm or leave to cool down.

Tip

Butter beans equal 7g nett carbs per 100g.
