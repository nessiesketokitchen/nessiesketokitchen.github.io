--- layout: post title: Garlic Paste date: 2018-03-08 12:52:34 -0200 categories: Recipes ---

This one is nice and easy, and you'll find yourself using it often.

Ingredients

  • 5 cloves garlic
  • Salt

Recipe


  1. Roughly chop the garlic, add salt and continue to chop it to a fine consistency.
  2. Once the pieces are small, start by flattening it with your blade and smoothing your blade over the garlic until it forms a paste.
