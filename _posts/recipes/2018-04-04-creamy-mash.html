--- layout: post title: Creamy Cauliflower Mash date: 2018-04-04 10:00:34 -0200 categories: Recipes ---

Forget about potato mash, this will knock your socks off!

Ingredients

Yield: Enough for 4.

  • 50ml butter
  • 1 small clove garlic
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 big head cauliflower (about 600g)
  • 30ml stock (I prefer chicken but you can use any stock you like)
  • 125ml cream

Tip

If you want to change it up you can always use pumpkin, sweet potato or broccoli instead of cauliflower.

Recipe


  1. Chop the garlic and onions roughly or finely chop if you don't have a blender.
  2. Add 25ml of the butter to a hot pan (reserve the rest for later).
  3. Fry the garlic first for a minute and then add the onions and fry till soft on a medium heat. Season with salt.
  4. While the onions are frying chop up the cauliflower (the smaller the cauliflower is chopped the quicker it wil cook).
  5. Add the cauliflower and fry on a high heat till it burns slightly (this adds a nice charred flavour but it is optional). Season with salt.
  6. Add the stock and the cream, cover and let simmer for 10 minutes.
  7. Open the lid and let it reduce till it is thick and creamy.
  8. Take off the heat and add the remaining butter.
  9. Blend or mash by hand and serve.
  10. Taste and add seasoning as needed or more cream and butter if you want it to be creamier.

Enjoy!
