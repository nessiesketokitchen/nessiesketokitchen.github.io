Serve this at your next dinner party and impress your guests. The best thing about this sauce is it is quick and easy to make.

Prep time: 5 minutes
Cook time: 10-15 minutes
Serving: +-250ml

Ingredients

  • 3 medium plums (+-250g)
  • ½ cup erythritol
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce alternative
  • 2 star anise
  • 1 tsp orange zest

Instructions


  1. Finely dice the plums.
  2. Combine all the ingredients in a sauce pan and put it on a medium heat.
  3. Leave to simmer for 10-15 minutes, until the mixture thickens.
  4. Serve warm or room temperature.



Tip

Plums contains pectin that naturaly thickens the sauce. Best served with poultry but you're welcome to try it with any other meat.
