Did someone say grilled cheese?

Ingredients

Yield: 2-4 slices of bread (depends on how big the slices are shaped).

  • 600g cauliflower (cut up into chunks - the smaller, the quicker it will cook and the easier it will be to drain the water)
  • 1 large egg
  • 100g cheese (any you prefer - I like half cheddar, half parmesan but parmesan can be expensive so it's optional)
  • 1 tbsp psyllium husk
  • 1/4 tbsp dried italian mix herbs
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder

Recipe


  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Cook the cauliflower until soft - do not add water since cauliflower already has a lot of water in it.
  3. Let the cauliflower cool down and then transfer it to a cloth. Use the cloth to squeeze out all the water.
  4. Transfer the cauliflower to a bowl and mix in the rest of the ingredients.
  5. On a baking tray lined with baking paper, shape the mix into bread slices.
  6. Bake for about 10 minutes or till golden brown (every oven is differnt, you'll have to keep an eye on it).

Tip

Spices are completely optional and you can add whatever you prefer.

Enjoy!
