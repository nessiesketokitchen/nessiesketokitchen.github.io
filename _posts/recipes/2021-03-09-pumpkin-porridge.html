Are you an oats for breakfast fan? A warm, buttery hug in a bowl fan? Have you been missing it? Well look no futher. My Pumkin Porridge is the perfect replacement. The nuttiness of the almond flour inspires the same flavour profile as oats.

Prep time: 3 minutes
Cook time: 5-7 minutes
Serving: 4

Ingredients

  • 100g butter
  • ¼ cup almond flavour
  • ⅓ cup erythritol or other sugar substitute
  • 3 tbsp erythritol
  • 4 cups pumpkin puree
  • a pinch of salt
  • cinnamon to serve

Instructions


  1. Melt the butter on a medium heat in a pot.
  2. Mix in the almond flour and let cook for about 2-3 minutes, you want to be able to smell the nuttiness.
  3. Turn the heat up and add the pumpkin puree and cook till warmed through.
  4. Add the desired amount of erythritol and salt.
  5. Serve with cinnamon.

Tip

I cook my own pumpkin puree, four cups puree is equal to 1kg of pumpkin.
I cook my pumpkin in a bit of butter and salt, nothing more.
My preferred method for cooking pumpkin is steaming it for 10 minutes and then leaving it uncovered until soft to cook out all the moisture.
Pumpkin has a lot natural moisture, you don't need to add water to steam.
Add as much erythritol and butter as you want, the amounts in the recipe are to my liking.
