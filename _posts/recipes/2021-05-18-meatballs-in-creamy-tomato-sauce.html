This recipe is everything and more. It's a warm hug on a cold winter's night. Hearty, favourful and perfectly balanced.

Prep time: 20 minutes
Cook time: 45 minutes
Serving: 12 meatballs (4 servings)

Ingredients: Meatballs

  • 10g (handful) garlic chives
  • 10g (handful) parsley
  • 10g (handful) handful basil
  • 600g beef mince
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter for frying

Ingredients: Creamy tomato sauce

  • 1 large red pepper
  • 1 small onion
  • 50g butter
  • 2-3 cloves garlic
  • 500g Roma tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp erythritol
  • ½ cup beef stock
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp pepper
  • ½ cup grated parmesan
  • ½ cup cream

Instructions: Meatballs


  1. Finely chop all of the herbs.
  2. Combine all off the ingredients and mix together.
  3. Shape into 12 small sized (50g) meatballs.
  4. Fry the meatballs on a high heat for +- 3 minutes a side until golden brown.

Instructions: Creamy tomato sauce


  1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C and set to grill.
  2. Remove the meatballs from the pan and use the same pan to cook the sauce.
  3. Slice the pepper in half and put skin up in the oven. Leave it under the grill until charred (+- 10-15 minutes).
  4. Roughly chop the onion, melt the butter in a large sauce-pan and add the onions. Leave the onions to fry (+- 5 minutes) on a medium/low heat until soft and golden brown.
  5. Chop the garlic,tomatoes and grilled pepper and add it to the onions, let simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Add the rest of the ingredients and mix thoroughly until the parmesan has metled. Leave uncovered to simmer for +-15 minutes until the sauce has slighly thickend.
  7. Add the meatballs back into the pan and cook until heated through.
  8. Serve with side of choice, I served it with low carb pasta made from seed and nut flour.



Tip

If you don't have Roma tomatoes, use cherry tomatoes or add extra erythritol/sugar as the Roma tomatoes are sweeter.
