This pesto is actually better than your standard non-vegan variety. Not only is nutritional yeast super good for you, it's also super tasty. Once you try it you won't want to go back. I added parsley to the pesto to give it a peppery kick.

Prep time: 5 minutes
Cook time: 3-5 minutes
Serving: +- 15 tbsp servings

Ingredients

  • ½ cup pecan nuts
  • 2 cups basil leaves (fully packed)
  • ½ cup flatleaf parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 3 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • ½ - ¾ cup extra virgin olive oi
  • ¼ - ½ tsp sea salt/Himalayan sal

Instructions


  1. Heat up a frying pan and toast the pecan nuts for 3-5 minutes.
  2. Combine all the ingredients in a food processor/blender and blend until a slightly chunky paste forms.

Tip

Serve with crackers/nachos.
Add to wraps/burgers/omelettes, etc.
Add the olive oil and salt in small batches and, taste as you go.
