--- layout: post title: Cream Cheese date: 2018-03-08 10:45:13 -0200 categories: Recipes ---

Just as yummy as store-bought, and much cheaper.

Ingredients

Yield: +-500g of cream cheese.

  • 2L amasi
  • 1 tsp salt

Recipe


  1. Mix the amasi and salt.
  2. Cover a strainer with a cloth and put it over a bowl.
  3. Pour the amasi into the cloth covered strainer.
  4. Leave over-night in the fridge.
  5. Discard the liquid in the bowl and move the cream cheese from the strainer into a container.
