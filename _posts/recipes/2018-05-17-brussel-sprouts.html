Presenting brussel sprouts in a new light.

Ingredients

Yield: enough for 4.

Brussel Sprouts
  • 1 glove garlic
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 5 rashers bacon cut up or 100g bits of bacon
  • 2 small leeks
  • 350g brussel sprouts
Sauce
  • 250ml cream
  • 80g blue cheese (you can add more or less depending on how strong you want it)
  • 1 tsp honey or xylitol for lower carbs - this amount of honey shared between four won't do any harm
  • 1 tbsp pepper
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 tbsp Mayonnaise

Recipe


  1. Chop up the garlic, slice the leeks and cut the brussel sprouts into quarters.
  2. Fry the garlic in the butter with the pieces of bacon and leave it to crisp up.
  3. While you wait for the bacon to crisp up, pour the cream (for the sauce) into a separate pot and let it heat up but not boil.
  4. Once the bacon is crispy add the leeks and brussel sprouts and leave for about 10 minutes, I prefer my vegetables to still be crisp but you can leave it on longer.
  5. Once the cream is hot, leave it on the stove on a low heat and add the blue cheese piece by piece while stirring until it dissolves.
  6. Take the cream off the heat and add the honey/xylitol, pepper and salt. Only add the mayonnaise once it has cooled off a bit.
  7. Pour the sauce over the brussel sprouts or serve on the side.

Enjoy!
