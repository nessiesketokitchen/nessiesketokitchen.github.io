This recipe was a happy coincident. My gut was in need of some TLC but I was also craving something sweet and tasty, the rest is history. This drinkable PB&J is delicious, creamy and full of fiber, probiotics and good fats.

Prep time: 2 minutes
Cook time: 1 minute
Serving: 2x 275ml servings

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup frozen blackberries
  • 2 cups milk kefir
  • 2 small avocados (1 large)
  • 2 tbsp sugar-free syrup
  • 2 tsp sugar-free peanut butter

Instructions


  1. Combine all the ingredients and blend together.



Tip

I used a sugar-free Maple syrup for this recipe (made by a local company).
Any ful fat/double cream yoghurt will do if you don't have kefir available.
Substitute almond, coconut or macadamia nut milk for a dairy-free alternative.
