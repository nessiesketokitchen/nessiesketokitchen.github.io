No eggs? No butter? No problem! These brownies are life changing, this is one of my favourite recipes. They are decadent, soft and super gooey. The secret Ingredient? MAYO!!! You'll never make your brownies any other way.

Prep time: 10 minutes
Cook time: 25 minutes
Serving: 1x 24cm square tin/casserole dish.

Ingredients: gooey brownies

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 2 tbsp whey powder
  • 1 tsp phsylium husk
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ¼ cup erythritol
  • 2 tbsp cocoa power
  • ¼ boiling water
  • ¼ mayonnaise
  • ¾ cream

Ingredients: gooey brownies

  • ¼ cup cream
  • 100g sugar-free chocolate

Instructions: brownies


  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Boil water.
  3. Line the tin/dish with baking paper and grease as an extra precaution.
  4. Combine all the dry ingredients (except the cocao powder) in a mixing bowl.
  5. Combine the cocoa and boiling water in a separate bowl.
  6. Add the mayonnaise and cream to the dry ingredients and mix.
  7. Add the cocoa poder mixture and mix thoroughly.
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared tin/dish.
  9. Bake for 25 minutes.
  10. Let the brownies cool down in tin/dish.
  11. Top with sugar-free chocolate ganache (see instructions below).
  12. Use the baking paper to lift the brownies from the tin/dish.
  13. Cut into as many brownies as you please.
  14. Store in an airtight container, careful not to stack them.
  15. If your room temperature is hot, store it in the fridge. Remove a few mintues before eating.

Instructions: brownies


  1. Heat up the cream.
  2. Break up the chocolate and stir into the cream until completely melted.
  3. Pour on top of the cooled brownies.



Tip

These brownies don't contain any raw egg or flour so don't worry about under baking them.
The brownies will rise really high in the oven and then colapse because there are no eggs to form a structure but this adds to the gooeyness.
Handle the brownies with care. They are more delicate than normal brownies because they don't contain eggs or flour. I suggest moving them with a spatula.
