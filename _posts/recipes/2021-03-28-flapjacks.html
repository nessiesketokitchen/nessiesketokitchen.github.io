These flapjacks are sugar and gluten-free but still super soft and fluffy. You won't even notice the difference. The whey powder is a key ingredient in creating the soft and fluffy texture.

Prep time: 5 minutes
Cook time: 15 minutes
Serving: 21 flapjacks

Ingredients

  • 150g cream cheese
  • 4 extra-large eggs
  • 2 tbsp erythritol
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 2 tbsp erythritol
  • 2 tbsp whey powder

Instructions


  1. Combine all the ingredients except the whey powder in a bowl and whisk together.
  2. Sift the whey powder into the mixture and combine.
  3. Heat up a frying pan (on a low to medium heat) and add a little bit of butter.
  4. Pour the batter in small circular cakes into the pan.
  5. Flip it over when bubbles form and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 until the batter is finished.
