These Granola bars are the perfect addition to any lunch box. They make a fantastic snack and are loaded with good, healthy fats and fiber but most importantly they taste super good.

Prep time: 10 minutes
Fridge time: 30 minutes
Serving: 12 large rectangular bars/24 small square

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups granola
  • ½ cup peanut butter
  • ½ cup sugar-free syrup
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil

Instructions


  1. Line a small tray/dish (+- 26cm casserole dish) with baking paper.
  2. Mix together the granola, peanut butter and syrup.
  3. Melt the coconut oil and mix in the granola mixture.
  4. Add to the lined rectangular tray and press down.
  5. Put the tray in the fridge and leave for a minimum of 30 minutes.
  6. Remove from the fridge and tray and cut into desired size and shape.



Tip

The baking paper makes it easier to remove the bars from the tray.
Store the bars in an airtight container in the fridge.
The coconut oil serves as a binder for the bars.
I don't make my own granola because I don't eat it often enough, the granola used is low-carb and from a local company.
