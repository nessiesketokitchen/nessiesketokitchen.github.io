This cake is what winter dreams are made of. It's like a warm hug bursting with flavour.

Prep time: 15 minutes
Cook time: 25-30 minutes
Serving: 1x 24cm cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups almond/macadamia flour
  • ⅓ cup whey powder
  • ½ cup erythritol
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp ground cardamom
  • ½ tsp ground all spice
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 5 eggs
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • ½ cup cream or milk

Instructions


  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C and line a 24cm cake tin.
  2. Combine the dry ingredients in a mixing bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs until light and fluffy (+- 5 minutes).
  4. Combine the olive oil and cream/milk and add it to the dry ingredients.
  5. Mix a third of the whisked eggs into the cake mixture and then fold in the rest.
  6. Pour the batter into the lined cake tin and bake for 25-30 minutes.



Tip

If you don't want a big cardamom kick, add only ½ tsp cardamom.
Have the eggs whisked and ready to go before you combine the dry and wet ingredients to make the folding process easier. Whey powder is essential in creating a cake like texture but it becomes sticky when combined with wet ingredients.
After 25 mintues, test the doneness of the cake by inserting a knife or skewer. If it comes out clean it's ready but if it's still wet leave the cake and test in increments of 5 minutes. The cake starts to cook very quickly towards the end.
Use this Plum sauce recipe to serve with the cake. Omit the soy sauce and add 1 tsp vanilla extract.
