If you're looking for a dip that ticks all of the taste boxes, this dip is it. The umami of the smokey mayonnaise pairs perfectly with the natural sweetness of the red pepper, and is rounded of with the bitterness of tahini.

Prep time: 10 minutes
Cook time: 10-15 minutes
Serving: +- 10 tbsp servings

Ingredients

  • 2 large red peppers
  • 1 tbsp tahini
  • 1 tbsp smoked mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice

Instructions


  1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C and set to grill.
  2. Slice the peppers in half and, remove the core and any white flesh.
  3. Flatten the peppers on a baking tray and roast in the oven for 10-15 minutes. Don't be afraid to really char the peppers.
  4. Cover the peppers in a bowl and leave them to sweat for 5 minutes.
  5. Peel off the burnt skins.
  6. Put the peppers and the rest of the ingredients into a food processor/blender and blend until the desired smoothness.

Tip

Serve with crackers/nachos.
Add to wraps/burgers/omelettes, etc.
I use a store bought keto smoked mayonnaise but you can easily add liquid smoke to any mayonnaise.
