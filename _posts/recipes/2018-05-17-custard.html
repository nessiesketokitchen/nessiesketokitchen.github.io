Better than anything you'll find in the store.

Ingredients

Yield: +-500ml custard.

  • 250m ml cream
  • 300ml milk
  • 85ml xylitol or other sugar substitute
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1/2 tsp xantham gum (optional - depends on how thick you want it)

Recipe


  1. Heat cream and milk in sauce pan over medium heat.
  2. Mix yolks and sugar in a bowl.
  3. Slowly add the hot milk to the yolk mixture while stirring.
  4. Pour back into the pan and put on a low heat.
  5. Stir occasionally to ensure it does not curdle or burn at the bottom (you don't want to over stir otherwise it will become too frothy)
  6. Once the mix has thickend it is ready.
  7. If you want it to be thicker stir in the xantham gum and let it cook for about 2 minutes more.

Tip

Use the 'spoon test' to test if it is ready/thickend. If it coats the back of a spoon without running off it is ready.

Enjoy!
