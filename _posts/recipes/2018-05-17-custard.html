Better than anything you'll find in the store.

Prep time: 5 minutes
Cook time: 20-30 minutes
Serving: +-500ml

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • ⅓ cup erythritol or other sugar substitute
  • 5 large eggs
  • ½ tsp xantham gum (optional)

Recipe


  1. Heat the cream and milk in a heavy bottomed sauce-pan over a medium heat.
  2. Separate the yolks from the eggs.
  3. Mix the yolks and erythritol in a bowl.
  4. Slowly add the hot milk to the yolk mixture while whisking.
  5. Pour the mixture back into the pan and turn down the heat.
  6. Cook the custard low and slow for the best results.
  7. Stir occasionally (with a wooden spoon or spatula) to ensure it does not curdle or burn at the bottom
  8. Once the mix has thickend it is ready, use the spoon test.
  9. If you want the custard to be thicker, stir in the xantham gum and let it cook for another 2 minutes.

Tip

Spoon test: If the custard coats the back of a wooden spoon or spatula (without running off) it is ready. Custard that is thickened without starch is very fragile, take it slow otherwise it will curdle.

Enjoy!
