This is a low-carb lifestyle blog.

The aim of this blog is to share recipes that I've either created or modified to be low-carb friendly, tips, meal ideas and to support people in maintaining a low-carb lifestyle.

I am a qualified chef and I have a lot of low-carb knowledge, I have been following this lifestyle for about 2 years.

I work as a private chef and I run a low carb kitchen in Stellenbosch through which you can order meals, for more information check out the Facebook link below.
