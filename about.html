Why did I choose the ketogenic lifestyle as my main focus?
I've been following a ketogenic lifestyle since 2016. After leaving home studying and working took its toll on me and I started making very unhealthy choices. I gained 35kg in a very short period of time, things like walking a few steps left me breathless and my doctor warned me that I was pre-diabetic.
Drastic changes had to be made. I explored various diets and tested them all out but the ketogenic lifestyle is the one that stuck and made me feel incredible. It's only once I cut carbs that I discoverd my body doesn't digest them very well. Symptoms such as constant headaches and fatigue, bloating and other intestinal issues (issues I actually started struggling with in my teens but never even considered to be diet related) vanished.

Now I share my passion for this lifetsyle through my blog. All the recipes were either created or modified (from non-ketogenic recipes) by me.
