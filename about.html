I qualified as a chef and pastry chef in 2012. Although I loved working in the kitchen, my dream has always been to work online so that I could pursue my other passion: travelling the world. A dream I thought was silly because I'm a chef and how thus a chef work online? One good thing that came out of this pandemic is that it forced me re-evaluate my career option and that's when I discoverd that I could turn my love for developing and photographing recipes into a career.
Please follow me on my journey as I navigate this new chapter as an amateur food photographer.

I've been following a ketogenic lifestyle since 2016. After leaving home, studying and working took it's toll on me and I made very unhealthy choices. I gained 35kg in a very short period of time. I started struggling with various health issues. Things like walking a few steps left me breathless, I had pain and inflammation in my joints, I was always tired and my doctor warned me that I was pre-diabetic.
Drastic changes had to be made. I explored various diets and tested them all out but the ketogenic lifestyle is the one that stuck and made me feel incredible. It's only once I cut carbs that I discoverd my body doesn't digest them very well. I went from feeling bloated and swollen all the time, stomach cramps, headaches and fatigue to feeling the best I've ever felt. I grew up with a lot of these symptoms and back then, not knowing how important diet was I never thought it could be the food I was consuming that caused these problems.

This is a ketogenic lifestyle blog.
