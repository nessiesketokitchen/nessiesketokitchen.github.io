These pancakes are sugar and gluten-free but still super soft and fluffy. You won't even notice the difference. The whey powder is a key ingredient to creating soft pancakes. Another blogger served as an inspiration for the creation of these.

Prep time: 5 minutes
Cook time: 10-15 minutes
Serving: +- 21 flapjacks

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cup cream cheese
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp erythritol
  • 2 tbsp whey powder

Instructions


  1. Combine all the ingredients except the whey powder in a bowl and whisk together.
  2. Sift the whey powder into the mixture and whisk/blend together.
  3. Heat up a frying pan (on a low to medium heat) and add a little bit of butter.
  4. Pour the batter in small circular cakes into the pan.
  5. Flip the flapjacks over when bubbles form on the surface.



Tip

Keep a close eye on the heat of your pan, these flapjacks burn much quicker.
